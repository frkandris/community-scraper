About CommUnity

CommUnity started in 2025 with a simple goal: make it easier to find local hobby groups, clubs and communities without spending hours searching the web.

Every few hours an automated pipeline searches the web for community groups matching each city × interest combination. It downloads the relevant pages, runs a local AI language model to extract structured data — meeting schedules, locations, contacts, websites — and filters out results that aren't genuine joinable groups. No cloud AI services are used; everything runs on self-hosted, open-source infrastructure.

The goal is to eventually cover every major city in the world across dozens of interest categories, so anyone relocating or looking for new people can find their people in minutes.
